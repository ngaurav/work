Regressively Engineered Analysis through Cognitive Tests

REACT is a process of testing, analysis and feedback for students of class 3 to 9. We have been working very hard to achieve the pinnacle of evolution in assessment methods. We proudly present the exam which aims to deliver beyond evaluation, beyond analysis.

Story Behind REACT

Most of the schools are already encouraging national level tests, which aim to evaluate the skills of students. But there is a problem, which we would like to discuss. Have you ever wondered why the learning received by taking such tests, is not getting reflected elsewhere. The fact is: the effect which these tests have on students depends on their performance in the test. Our analysis has revealed that these effects can be broadly classified into 3 categories.

The students among top 5% in their schools get highly motivated when they see themselves among top 100 students in the city. They gain a boost in their confidence.

Some other students from the same top 5%, who could not perform upto their full potential on that day (despite of being equally talented) get demotivated, and feel that these tests are don't help in improving their scholastic skills. So, most of them do not participate the second time.

Students below top 5% who took the same test, continue taking these tests but they don't hope for any gains or rewards. They do it for fun because they like the carefully designed interesting questions. It is refreshing to learn something out of your curriculum. All 3 categories of students are not making the best out of their investment. Lets revisit the 3 categories again, but in reverse order.

The students who take these tests only because they enjoy the experience, are actually the ones who can make the most out of these tests just because of their sheer interest. They are average students. The one thing that they take way from these tests is new brain-tickling questions. But this comes at the cost of their confidence. After taking the tests they don't get to learn how to solve those interesting questions. Even remembering the exact question is difficult. But one thing which settles in their mind that there were so many question which left them clueless.

The top 5% students who got didn't find the tests useful are left with a bad impression about such tests which inhibits their growth in future. There are some tests which are intentionally made tough by just asking questions from one standard above. They are most harmful types of tests. One should never take such tests unless there are monetary rewards ;)

The top 5% students who performed well, get only confidence and motivation as a reward. But actually this is the only category which spend time, money and energy in doing questions, one could already do. It is hard to realise that they are the biggest losers here.

This is what urged us to build REACT.

What Students Get?

With REACT we have raised the bar so high that it is impossible for others to meet our benchmarks. And we were capabale of doing so because we ourselves have been a part of this education system and as a student we all have excelled throughout. After all this experience, we have designed the state-of-art assessment method which offers you:

Tailored Questions

With immense effort we have compiled a curated set of questions designed to put your cognitive abilities to test. We have spent equal efforts in designing the options, every detail you miss will lead you to one of the incorrect options. Our questions test at least 2 concepts from more than one subject. Each question simulates real world problems which can be solved using academic knowledge imparted in schools.

Line-Of-Thought Solutions

Firstly, we are providing solutions. Secondly, you might feel that solution is so hard that one cannot think about it in a time-constrained test, so, we are providing you with line-of-thought which will teach you how to break the question in simpler questions and identify the most difficult step. Lastly, we will also provide you with some shortcuts(if any) to solve the question without actually knowing the concept.

Predictive Analysis

By the time you get our solutions you will not remember how you approached every question in the test. For each wrong answer we have designed our options such that we can guess what mistake you made. Also, we will provide you insights about your performance with respect to other students. Our analysis will be more focused on the skills of your brain, rather than the knowledge of subjects and how to develop lacking skills.
